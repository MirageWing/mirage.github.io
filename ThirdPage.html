Website Development [INSTAGRAHAM.INC]

INSTAGRAHAM.INC is a fictional social media company. Student is requested to design a social media website for user to upload pictures (.png, .jpg, .gif...) to the website and allows others to view and collect the images while the owners can edit detail of the images.

Development

This project is developed and written with PHP in NETBEAN IDE. Database is constructed with MySQL.

Video Editing [Strategise Your Future]

[Strategise Your Future] is an educational video providing tips and advice for teenagers in pathing their future careers. The video is presented in a narrative method where a main character is regreting on his decision when deciding a career. The story took an unexpected turn when he time travelled back to his highschool age and took tips and advice in an educational video.

Development

SOFTWARE used to edit the video includes Adobe After Effect and Adobe Premiere Pro.
